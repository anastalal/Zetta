Why Us?

Why Choose Zetta Technologies?

With Zetta Technologies, using DaaS model, customer doesn’t have to make large upfront investments in hardware, software, and infrastructure. Instead, they pay a predictable monthly fee to access the latest devices and technologies without having to worry about depreciation or the costs associated with owning and managing devices.

  • Flexibility

  • Cost efficiency and financial control

  • Simplicity

  • Vendor Relation

How does Zetta work?

  • Select

    Select your preferred device and plan duration: 12, 18, 24, or 36 months.

  • Subscribe

    Sign up and onboard seamlessly with our E-KYC process.

  • Receive

    Receive and activate your devices with seamless setup to get you up and running in no time.

  • Pay

    Enjoy effortless transactions for your subscription plan, available in monthly or quarterly payments.

  • Return or upgrade

    At the end of your plan, you have the option to either return the devices or upgrade.
